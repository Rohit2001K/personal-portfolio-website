Home Page

This is the starting point of our website, offering easy navigation and an engaging layout to help you explore all the key features and content.

Login/Signup Page

This page allows users to either log in with their existing credentials (username and password) or create a new account by submitting their username, email, and password (twice for confirmation). The intuitive design ensures a smooth and secure user experience for both returning and new users.

Market Page and Pagination

On this page, users can browse a list of the latest products. To view more details, they are prompted to log in. The page features a dynamic search function and automatic pagination, allowing users to easily navigate through product listings with "Next" and "Previous" buttons, ensuring a seamless browsing experience.

Searching

The search functionality allows users to find products by main tags, sub-tags, or categories. This intuitive search system helps users quickly narrow down their options and find exactly what they're looking for, offering a streamlined and efficient browsing experience.

Product Info

This page provides detailed information about a product, including images, title, description, ownership type, purchase date, and more. At the bottom, users can view the lister's profile and have the option to send a private message, facilitating easy communication between buyers and sellers.

Inbox And Messages

Inox is a messaging platform that allows users to easily view and respond to recent messages. With Inox, you can directly contact sellers by navigating to any product and sending them a direct message (DM) for seamless communication.

Upload Product / Edit

With the 'Upload Product' feature, users can easily list their products by clicking the 'Sell' button in the navigation bar. Simply fill in the product details, upload images and tags, and submit. To view and manage your listings, head to the 'My Products' section, where you can update or edit your posted items
